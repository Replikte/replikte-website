Privacy policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026 · Replikte LLC · Boca Raton, Florida, USA

This policy describes how Replikte LLC (“Replikte,” “we,” “us”) handles information in connection with this public marketing website (the “Site”). It does not govern services we deliver under a separate client agreement; those engagements are covered by their own terms and privacy commitments.

Who we are

Replikte LLC is a Florida limited liability company. For privacy questions: contact@replikte.com.

What this Site does not do

The Site is primarily informational. We do not operate a login or a customer account system on this Site, and we do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal data through the Site itself.

Information you send us

If you email us or include personal details in a scheduling or contact flow, we use that information only to respond and to operate our business, including evaluating whether we can help with your inquiry.

Third-party services

  • Hosting. This Site may be hosted on infrastructure that processes technical data (for example, IP address, browser type, date/time of requests) in server or CDN logs. That processing is governed by the host’s policies (for example, GitHub Pages / GitHub privacy documentation if applicable).
  • Scheduling. “Book a call” links open our booking page on Neo Space (book.neo.space), a third-party scheduling service. If you book a call, that provider’s privacy policy applies to information you submit there.
  • Fonts. We load fonts from Google Fonts; Google may receive technical information such as IP address and user agent when your browser requests those assets. See Google’s privacy documentation for details.
  • Social networks. Links to LinkedIn or X leave our Site; those platforms have their own privacy practices.

Cookies

This Site does not set first-party analytics cookies by default. Third parties you interact with (for example, the scheduling provider) may set cookies according to their own policies.

Retention

We retain business correspondence and scheduling-related records as needed to operate Replikte and meet legal obligations. Retention periods vary by record type.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of personal information, or to object to processing. To exercise rights or ask questions, contact us at contact@replikte.com. We may need to verify your request.

International visitors

Replikte is based in the United States. If you visit from another country, your information may be processed in the U.S. or other locations where we or our service providers operate.

Children

The Site is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Site.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
