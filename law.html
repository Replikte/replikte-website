The stakes are different in law

In most businesses, a dropped ball costs money. In a law firm, it can cost a career.

A missed statute of limitations. A filing deadline that slipped. A client who felt ignored long enough to file a bar complaint. These aren't hypotheticals — they're the outcomes that end practices.

The attorneys who lose their licenses rarely lose them for bad legal work. They lose them for the things that fall through the cracks when a small firm is running on memory, goodwill, and whoever happens to be in the office that day.

What we build removes that exposure. Not because we practice law — we don't touch the legal work — but because the system that tracks your deadlines doesn't have bad weeks, doesn't forget, and doesn't call in sick the day before a filing is due.

On confidentiality: We work within your existing tools and protocols. No client data sits in third-party AI systems without your explicit review and approval. We treat attorney-client privilege the same way you do — as non-negotiable.
